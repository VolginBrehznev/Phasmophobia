Deogen

- During a hunt, the Deogen moves at a very fast speed when not in line-of-sight with a player. If it has line-of-sight with a player, the Deogen appears to have several speed brackets, moving slower the closer the player is; minimum speeds of 0.4 m/s have been recorded.

- The Deogen's hunt sanity threshold has been observed to be 40%, instead of the usual 50%.

- The Deogen has a unique spirit box sound that sounds like heavy breathing.
