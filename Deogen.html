Deogen

- The Deogen's speed is based on its pathfinding distance to the target player and has three thresholds: above 6 meters, between 6 and 2.5 meters and below 2.5 meters.
> 6 meters: 3 m/s
< 2.5 meters: 0.4 m/s

- The Deogen can produce a unique response to any question, which is constant heavy bull-like breathing that lasts 3-4 seconds.

- The Deogen's hunt sanity threshold has been observed to be 40%, instead of the usual 50%.

- The Deogen will always have Spirit Box as one of its two evidences on Nightmare difficulty.
