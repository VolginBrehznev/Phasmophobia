Moroi

- The Moroi has an ability to "curse" a target; this is likely through the player asking a question and obtaining a response from the spirit box. The curse causes the target's sanity to drop faster while in the investigation area

- When smudged during a hunt, the duration where the ghost cannot see nor kill players is observed to be 12 seconds (twice as long as the usual 6 seconds), though the exact value has not been confirmed.

- The Moroi has been observed to be faster during hunts the lower the average sanity, with recorded base speeds of approximately 1.4 m/s at 45% average sanity, and 2.1 m/s at 0% average sanity.[1] The ghost appears to speed up with line-of-sight of a player.
