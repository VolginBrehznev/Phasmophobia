Moroi

- The Moroi supposedly has an ability to place a "curse" on a target. When the curse is placed, the player's sanity will drain faster under some conditions. Sanity pills are speculated to remove this curse, while the curse will temporarily pause while outside the investigation area.

- When smudged during a hunt, the duration where the ghost cannot see nor kill players is observed to be 12 seconds (twice as long as the usual 6 seconds), though the exact value has not been confirmed.

- The Moroi has been observed to be slightly faster during hunts the lower the average sanity.[verify]
